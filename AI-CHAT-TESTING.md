# AI Chat Widget - Quick Test Questions

Use these questions to test your AI chat widget after deployment:

## Basic Information Questions

- "What are your hours?"
- "Where are you located?"
- "What's your phone number?"
- "Do you take walk-ins?"
- "Do I need an appointment?"

## Service Questions

- "What services do you offer?"
- "Do you do gel nails?"
- "What's the difference between acrylic and dip powder?"
- "How long do gel nails last?"
- "Do you offer pedicures?"
- "What spa packages do you have?"
- "Do you do waxing?"

## Style Recommendation Questions

- "I'm going to a beach wedding, what should I get?"
- "I want something elegant for a job interview"
- "What's trending in nail art right now?"
- "I'm wearing a red dress to a party, what nails would match?"
- "I want something simple and professional"

## Pricing Questions

- "How much is a manicure?"
- "What's the price for gel nails?"
- "Do you have any packages?"

## Policy Questions

- "What payment methods do you accept?"
- "Can I bring my own polish?"
- "What are your safety practices?"
- "Do you offer services for men?"

## Expected AI Behavior

✅ **Good Responses:**

- Friendly and professional tone
- Accurate business information
- Encourages calling for specific pricing
- Provides helpful style recommendations
- Mentions the phone number: 714-820-6172

❌ **Red Flags:**

- Incorrect hours or location
- Made-up pricing
- Unprofessional tone
- Generic responses not specific to K&T Nails

## Testing Checklist

- [ ] Chat widget appears in bottom-right corner
- [ ] Widget opens/closes smoothly
- [ ] Messages send and receive properly
- [ ] Loading animation shows while waiting
- [ ] AI responses are relevant and accurate
- [ ] Mobile responsive (test on phone)
- [ ] Icons render correctly (sparkles, send button)
- [ ] Scrolling works in chat window
- [ ] Enter key sends messages
- [ ] No console errors (F12 to check)

## Performance Metrics

- **Response Time**: Should be 1-3 seconds
- **Accuracy**: AI should know all business details
- **Tone**: Friendly, helpful, professional
- **Relevance**: Answers should be specific to K&T Nails

---

**Pro Tip:** Test from different devices (desktop, mobile, tablet) to ensure consistent experience!
