FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our services, appointments, and salon policies.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome! However, we recommend calling ahead at 714-820-6172 to ensure availability, especially during weekends and peak hours.

What are your safety and sanitation practices?

Your safety is our top priority. We follow strict sanitation protocols including:

  • All tools are sterilized using hospital-grade disinfectants
  • Single-use files and buffers for each client
  • Pedicure tubs are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected between clients
  • Licensed technicians trained in proper hygiene practices
  • Clean linens and towels for every service
How long do gel nails last?

Gel manicures typically last 2-3 weeks with proper care, while gel pedicures can last 3-4 weeks. The longevity depends on nail growth rate and daily activities. We recommend returning for maintenance every 2-3 weeks for optimal results.

What's the difference between acrylic and dipping powder?

Both create durable, long-lasting nails, but they differ in application:

Acrylic: Mixed liquid and powder applied with a brush. Great for length, very durable, and customizable.

Dipping Powder: Nails are dipped into colored powder. Odorless, lighter feel, and typically lasts 3-4 weeks. Many find it gentler on natural nails.

Do you offer services for men?

Absolutely! We offer manicures, pedicures, and grooming services for everyone. Many of our male clients enjoy our spa pedicure packages for relaxation and foot care.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), and digital payment methods. Gratuity can be added to card payments or given in cash.

Can I bring my own nail polish?

Yes! While we have an extensive collection of colors and brands, you're welcome to bring your own polish. Please note that we cannot guarantee the same longevity with outside products.

What should I do to prepare for my appointment?

To get the most from your service:

  • Remove any existing polish before arriving (optional - we can do this for you)
  • Avoid shaving legs 24 hours before a pedicure to prevent irritation
  • Arrive a few minutes early to relax and choose your colors
  • Communicate any allergies or sensitivities to your technician
